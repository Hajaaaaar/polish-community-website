Celebrate Polish Heritage in South Wales

Join us at our upcoming Polish community events to experience the vibrant culture, rich traditions, and delicious cuisine that Poland has to offer. From lively music festivals and traditional food markets to thought-provoking film screenings and festive folk dance performances, there’s something for everyone. Discover the warmth of Polish hospitality, engage in cultural workshops, and enjoy a truly immersive experience that brings the spirit of Poland to Cardiff. Don’t miss out—mark your calendars and come celebrate with us!
