Health And Social Care Information

This page is dedicated to providing you with the vital role of health and social care services in supporting the well-being and integration of migrants in wales and the UK. Having this easy access to healthcare can help to feel supported and integrated in new communities. This page covers the crucial and diverse healthcare needs and support mechanisms, including the importance of tailored services and any challenges one may be facing when dealing with the healthcare system. The importance of mental health and the need for compassionate care is also vital, so we hope to provide useful information to make healthcare easy, more accessible and welcoming for all.
